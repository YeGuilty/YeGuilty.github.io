Wylia's Shortsword


      Wylia wields a simple shortsword. Its full-tang steel blade is about three-and-a-half feet long, double-edged; its hilt, of hardwood. By its construction, it may be considered a warknife. The scabbard is carved out of wood and wrapped with leather; the chape and locket are of metal. The sword lacks a guard.
